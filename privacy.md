  # UmpCall — Privacy Policy

  **Last updated:** June 6, 2025

  ## Overview
  UmpCall does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

  ## Data Collection
  UmpCall collects **no data** from its users. The app:
  - Requires no internet connection
  - Makes zero network calls
  - Has no analytics or tracking
  - Has no login or user accounts
  - Has no advertising

  ## Local Storage
  UmpCall stores your preferences (nickname, theme, counter state) locally on your device only. This
  data never leaves your device.

  ## Photo Library Access
  UmpCall requests photo library access solely to allow you to select custom coin face images. These
  images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

  ## Children's Privacy
  UmpCall does not collect any data from any user, including children.
