How it works

  • Past matches — green footer on home hero; separate tap from Start Match

  • Live section — pinned at top with green card; scorers see resume ▶
  • Finished — grouped by Today / Yesterday / This week / Month
  • Summary — shows “3 of 12 shown” when filters are active
