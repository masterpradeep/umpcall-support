## 3. Accounts and club access

- **No email login** — cloud features use an anonymous session on your device.
- **Club roles** (admin, scorer, viewer) are assigned by **which join code** you enter, not by self-selection.
- **Club owners** should save their **Club ID** and **scorer join code** in a safe place. Recovery on a new phone requires both (Settings → Club → Connect → **Recover**).
- You are responsible for keeping join codes confidential within your league.
- If you recover admin access on a new device, any previous admin session for that club may be demoted to scorer.

### Access codes — treat as confidential (club responsibility)

Club access is controlled by **codes you share**, not by email login:

| Code | Who should have it | Risk if leaked |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Scorer join code** | Trusted scorers | Can edit rosters and score for your club |
| **Viewer join code** | Read-only members | Can view club rosters and match history |
| **Club ID + scorer code** | Club owner only (recovery) | Can reclaim admin on a new device |
| **Live match code** | Spectators you invite | Anyone can follow that match’s scoreboard |
| **Handover pass code** | Substitute scorer only | Can take over scoring mid-match |

**Treat every code like a password.** Share only with people in your league who need that role. Do **not** post codes in public WhatsApp groups, social media, screenshots, or open channels.

**If a code is leaked or misused**, someone with that code may view or change your club or match data (within the permissions of that code). **Your club and its administrators are responsible** for protecting codes and for the consequences of sharing or failing to keep them confidential. GullyDev is **not liable** for unauthorized access caused by codes your league chose to share, published, or lost — except where applicable law requires otherwise.
