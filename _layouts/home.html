How club sync works

  1. Create or join a club with a join code in Settings.
  2. Score a match — rosters and scores sync to your league.
  3. Hand over to another scorer with a pass code if needed.

Keep codes confidential. Join codes and live match codes work like passwords — your club is responsible if they are shared publicly. See Terms §3.
